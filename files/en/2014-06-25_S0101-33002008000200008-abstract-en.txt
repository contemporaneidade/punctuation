The article aims to apprehend the deep social  political  economic and cultural transformations that characterized the three last decades  in contrast with the prosperity period following the Second World War  Therefore  the author critically analyses three specific works that deal with that period   The economics of global turbulence  by Robert Brenner  The long Twentieth Century  by Giovanni Arrighi  and The conditions of postmodernity  by David Haravey  The metatheoric procedure adopted allows him to indicate the requisites of an adequate critical theory of contemporary capitalism
