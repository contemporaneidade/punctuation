This paper proposes  from an ethnographic description of social organization and how it structures the local political authority  to analyze the connections between local power and public administration through the building of local authority at Aventureiro Beach  Ilha Grande  state of Rio de Janeiro  The inhabitants  political power and legitimate authority are connected to the ownership of boats and to the most successful campings  The boat owners take the role of legitimate at the negotiation with the municipality and state  looking after the inhabitants  rights  assuming a role in representing the domestic organization  families and vicinal groups  in political relations with the state
