The subject of this essay is the dilemmas of modernization such as viewed by S rgio Buarque de Holanda and Edward C  Banfield in Ra zes do Brasil and The moral basis of a backward society  The comparative analysis searches to elucidate the concepts of cordiality and amoral familism as stated by the authors  When referred to their own theoretical models  such concepts are described as expressions of private ethic traditions which are not compatible with the social ethos demanded for the entrance in the modern age  Within this scope  the conclusion is that Banfield s vision states that a private ethic tradition shall be annulled by the modernizing action  whereas S rgio Buarque de Holanda seeks to reconcile them by setting a balance between tendencies which are apparently antagonistic
