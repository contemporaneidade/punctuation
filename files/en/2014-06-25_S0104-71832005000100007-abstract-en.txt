In this article  I investigate the institution of Goi s as a historical and touristic town  interweaving discoursives series that gives visibility and meanings to the town by working the relations of urban space  time and the history  the delimitation of the town as a cultural values through the incorporation of the National Patrimony discourse  the invention of local traditions promoted by Organiza o Vilaboense de Artes e Tradi es  the production of the town  Patrimony of Humanity  in the Dossier of Goi s and the writing of Cora Coralina s memory that takes the form of the past inscribed in the texture of the town material
