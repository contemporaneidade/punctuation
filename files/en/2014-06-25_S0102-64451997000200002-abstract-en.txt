Against the mere multiplication of empirical case studies and considering the distintion between the actual and the possible the author defends the use of a mix of economic  sociological and political theoretical approaches in order to explain the attraction exercised by some development policy paradigms  without fear of  grand theory  The idea of coerence between political concepts  development strategies and economic orientations is systematically applied in the study of their adjustments regarding the international monetary order and the hegemonic system  Globalization is conceived in its scope and in its limit  both given by the finite resources of the planet Earth
