The article reports on sociological reflections about the uses of dramaturgical analysis in contemporary social sciences  which are based on extensive experience of drama teaching at the University of Bras lia  It also searches to reveal the affinities between Sociology and the scenic arts  by the use of the theatrum mundi metaphor applied to different sociological subjects  It presents an updated exegesis of the dramaturgical analysis perspective and shows its path on contemporary studies about performance in life  in culture and on stage
