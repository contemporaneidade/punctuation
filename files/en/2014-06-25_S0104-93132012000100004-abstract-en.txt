While discussing images of Our Lady and of the mothers and women of the Garden of Flowers  or  8220 Devils 8217  Hole 8221  a peripheral district of a city in the interior of S o Paulo state  this paper intends to explore the specificity of dramatic aesthetics  Rather than depend upon the aesthetics of social drama  as discussed in the works of Victor Turner  the present paper deals with a concept of montage that is inspired by the cinema of Sergei Eisenstein in order to illuminate a selection of field notes  and  along with Eisenstein  Julia Kristeva  Walter Benjamin  Michael Taussig and Antonin Artaud  Wit regards to those readers interested in hearing what these women may have to say  caution is suggested  for the words of these women may ring in our ears as the sounds of musket shots
