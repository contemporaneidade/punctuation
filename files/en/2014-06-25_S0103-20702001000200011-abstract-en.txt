Os sert es has been amply discussed in various fields of interpretation in Brazil  be it in literature  sociology  anthropology or history  This article aims to demonstrate that the means of characterization of the social existence of the peasant from the arid countryside has turned this work of Euclides da Cunha s into a landmark in the formation of the social sciences in Brazil  Based on Spencerian evolutionism  the author analyzes the  mis direction of social change in the country  as a means to contribute towards the reflection on Brazilian socio cultural singularities
