The aim of this article is to convey a type of sociological thinking still not much spread in the southern Brazilian state of Rio Grande do Sul  sociology of experience  theorized by French sociologist Fran ois Dubet  The effort is aimed at deepening the understanding of the concepts developed by that author  not only from the theoretical point of view  but also regarding their possibilities for application to the empirical field  That is why an analysis of two of his works will be carried out  Sociologie de l Exp rience  Sociology of Experience  e Dans quelle soci t  vivons nous   Which society do we live in  in an attempt to understand society s contemporary expressions  in its apparent crisis of values  ambiguities and incoherencies  The reflection will lead to a set of questions that remain open  among which  how does Sociology of Experience concern Brazilian society
