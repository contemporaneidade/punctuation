ABSTRACT History blocked by Capital and power has turned everyday life into a refuge from the disenchantment of an unlikely future  The big struggles in order to redeem humankind from its limits and miseries are being readapted to this new territory of life and living  Society is being reinvented  and consequently sociology is also experiencing a process of reinvention  In this context the mediation of knowlegde of the day by day gets a new importance in the construction of social relations
