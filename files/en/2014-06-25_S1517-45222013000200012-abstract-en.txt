In the past few years  mainly due the prominence that the Post Colonial studies have assumed in the field of sociology  several studies have pointed to the need to problematize the relations of production and circulation of sociological theory between center and periphery  Jos  Maur cio Domigues s book  Theory and Semi Periphery  by bringing to the center of theoretical reflections authors from different peripheral regions of the globe   such as Latin America  India  China and Israel   opens new possibilities for critical reflection about the geopolitics of sociological theory as well contributes to the discussion on global modernity from the Periphery and Semi Periphery
