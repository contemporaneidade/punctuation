This is the second part of the author s article dealing with WTO s new themes  MERCOSUL is positioning itself to start negotiations in several fronts  inside WTO in the next round of multilateral negotiations  with the EU in a inter regional agreement and with NAFTA inside the FTAA  Two themes are relevant to these negotiations  environment and labor standards  This article analyses the evolution of these themes in the international context from the Havana Charter  through UNCTAD  OECD and ILO to arrive in the WTO  The objective of this article is to give some elements for the discussion of these two themes in Mercosul s future negotiations
