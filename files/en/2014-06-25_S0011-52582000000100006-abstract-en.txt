In an exploration of aspects of soccer and nation in Brazil  the article demonstrates how soccer serves to organize and classify reality by dividing the world into clan teams  Approaching soccer as more than a metaphor  the article analyzes soccer teams as clans that are parts of a specific universe  where people are divided according to their support for a given team  As a system for classifying reality within people 146 s day to day lives  this universe brings people together and transforms vertical relationships into horizontal ones  Lastly  the article analyzes the relation between this type of classification and the construction of the Brazilian nation  The main purpose is to propose new issues and approaches pertinent to a sociological reading of Brazilian soccer
