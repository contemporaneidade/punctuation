In this work we develop a research in order to deepen our understanding of the operation of democracy within the solidarity economy  While a segment of academic literature production associations oppose the productive capitalist enterprise and deepen democracy  the other side rejects those possibilities  Denying this binary  through qualitative analysis  working with nine groups in the metropolitan area of  8203 8203 Porto Alegre  we conclude that there is some hybridism in relation to these practices with a weak and non deterministic trend to vertizalization and hierarchization that is caused by several factors
