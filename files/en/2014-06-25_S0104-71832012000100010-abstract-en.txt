This article discusses the relationship between ethnography and witchcraft in Jeanne Favret Saada s Les mots  la mort  les sorts  Deadly words  Its first aim is to present the main themes and concepts proposed by the author s description of witchcraft  As such  its purpose is to offer a commentary on the book by drawing attention to the various possibilities that it provides for an  anthropology of witchcraft  Among these  the article highlights the author s use of what we could call a  procedure of equivalence  or  principle of symmetry  namely the mode through which ethnography and witchcraft  when located on the same  plane of immanence  can describe each other in a reciprocal relationship of transformation
