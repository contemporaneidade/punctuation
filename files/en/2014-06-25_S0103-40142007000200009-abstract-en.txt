In this paper we will try to report the Brazilian micronutrients status  as well as in worldwide  specifically for minerals  Minerals have major importance on human body  becoming indispensable for the development and health of individuals  There is not yet an integral assessment of micronutrient status in the Brazilian subjects  but there are some studies pointing to the need of observation of alimentary tendencies that might lead to deficiencies  with adverse consequences to the population s health and the development of our country
