This work reflects about the notions of conception  substance and personhood among the the Shavante Indians  and between the G  Indians  in general  and its perception of the Other  What I try to demonstrate is that such notions  although it seems to  are not static  but extremely dynamic   being that such dynamism reflects an ideology of inclusive perception of this  Other  beyond bodiness  but also in its mythology  history and in the contact with the whites
