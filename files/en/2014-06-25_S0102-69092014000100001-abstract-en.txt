Interview by Sharon Zukin  given on August 2013 when in Sao Paulo  participating at the international conference  8220 Cities and Architectures  between heritage and memory 8221   FAU USP  Zukin addresses the New York context  highlighting her most recent book  Naked City  2010  In it  she makes a careful observation of the results of the widespread interventions of re urbanization  analyzes the concept of urban authenticity  and presents a deeper critical view over the influence of Jane Jacobs in the studies and political conflicts related to the urban way of life in the United States  The sociologist also examines the relationship between the big museums with the city and explains her recent research in Amsterdam and Shanghai  in which new themes are incorporated in her reflections
