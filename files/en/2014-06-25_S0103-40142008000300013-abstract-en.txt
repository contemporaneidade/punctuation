Due to reasons out of the scope of this article  important investments in the electric sector were delayed  increasing the risk of power shortages in Brazil in the near future  with serious impacts on industrial production and quality of life  Among the projects being studied by the Ministry of Energy to be operative in time to reduce that risk are the Angra III nuclear power plant and some hidro power plants in Northern Brazil  what raises doubts and provokes debates in the press  chiefly about the costs of the energy produced  In this article we try clear up some of these doubts
