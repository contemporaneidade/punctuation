Internet is considered an alternative source to study the history  mainly the history of diplomatic relations  The sites of the ministries of external relations around the world are analized  some attribute great importance to the history  with detailed retrospective analysis  Brazil  France  Mexico  Russia  Germany  United States  others do a selective analysis  or restricted to certain periods  Spain  Argentina  Italy  Belgium  Portugal  China  Japan  some emphasize the past  only showing historical information that are indispensable to the national image  Switzerland  and others show their history turned to the future  Britain
