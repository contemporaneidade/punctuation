This article searches to make a pictorial analysis of a selected range of visual documents about the carioca writer J lia Lopes de Almeida  1862 1934  with a view to identifying the possible dialogues among these images and the way in which they describe  pictorially  even though backwardly  the link between the writer and belletrism  These pictorial documents were taken from her personal archive  among which we can find some photographs   some of which were published on newspapers of her time   three artistic portraits and two drawings  This article reveals  not only the fact that J lia Lopes held the Art in high esteem  that is  in all its sensitive organizations  but it also has as its background the ample dialogue that these images establish with one of her most recurrent themes  that is  the feminine literary professionalization of the between centuries  19th  20th
