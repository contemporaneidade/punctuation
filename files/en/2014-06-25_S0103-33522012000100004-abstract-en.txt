In Brazil s last presidential campaign  abortion became a centerpiece of political debate  As a subject interconnecting the political and religious  public and private arenas  abortion was a strong point of debates on media coordinated through the internet  The aim of this paper is to examine the trajectory of a critical event in particular  the news of an alleged miscarriage by Mrs  M nica Serra  the wife of one of the presidential candidates  through newspapers  blogs  social media and Twitter  I intend to investigate how those several online and offline spaces of interaction and transmission of facts and versions coordinate from a subject that  included in the political struggle  comes to critically interconnect the dimensions of the public and the private
