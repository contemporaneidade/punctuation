This paper analyses the history of women embroiderers from Ibitinga  a small town in the state of S o Paulo  which is known by the beauty of its needlework  It recreates  from a historical perspective  the diffusion of the embroidery industry in the town  emphasizing the struggle of the worker s union to improve labor conditions and register workers at the Ministry of Labor  The study emphasizes the importance of worker s organization to decrease homework  as well as to legalize the industries  a process that lies at the roots of its vigorous growth during recent years
