Neo institutionalism is not a unified current of thought  On the contrary  at least three different methods of analysis emerged in this area in the last 25 years  historical institutionalism  rational choice institutionalism  and sociological institutionalism  All of them deal  from different angles  with the role performed by institutions in the determination of social and political results  The genesis as well as the peculiarities of these variants of  neo institutionalism  are exposed and examined
