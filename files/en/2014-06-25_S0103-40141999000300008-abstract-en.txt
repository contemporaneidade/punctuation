PTE LU S Gonzaga das Virgens was one of four people executed  hanged and quartered  on Nov  8  1799  in the Brazilian city of Salvador  Bahia  Among the three others  Lucas Dantas de Amorim Tores  Manuel Faustino dos Santos Lira and Jo o de Deus do Nascimento  he stood out for his higher education level  On his mother s side  he was a descendant of Portuguese and creoles  on his father s side  of creoles  A practicing Catholic  he nursed and made manifest a rebellious feeling against the predominant color prejudice of his day
