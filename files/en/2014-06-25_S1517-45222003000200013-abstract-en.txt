This article deals with the themes related to the construction of generational dynamics and the memory work activated by certain agents on a specific historical moment  In order to do that  a brief bibliographical discussion is firstly proposed on the notions of  generation  and  memory  specially on their heuristic potential  Afterwards  an analysis is developed of a group of narratives and tributes centered on the leftist youth s mobilization within Rio Grande de Sul s MDB  Brazilian Democratic Movement  in the  struggle against the dictatorship   specially the examination of state parliament special session as a posthumous tribute to sociologist and political leader Andr  C  Forster  Based on thos e two moments we intend to discuss the operational use of some categories to understand historical moments and the characters that animate them
