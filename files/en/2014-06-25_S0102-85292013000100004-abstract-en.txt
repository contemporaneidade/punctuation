Unlike research on the paradiplomacy of the southern states of Brazil  which has already produced significant literature  only a limited number of texts deal with the greater role of subnational entities in the Amazon region  This article introduces this discussion following two main axes  greater integration of state and local governments in international affairs during the deepening of globalization  and the relationship of these new agents with the foreign ministry and the Presidency  Thus  it intends to contribute to expanding the debate on this issue among international relations researchers  and presents an overview of recent paradiplomacy initiatives in the Amazon border states  more specifically Acre  Amazonas  Amap  Par  Roraima and Rond nia
