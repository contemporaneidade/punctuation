This article discusses the concepts of  modernity  and  post modernity  and rejects the notion that the agenda of modernity  linked to the notions of progress  education and scientific development  is exhausted  Brazil still has a modernization agenda to fulfill  which depends on its ability to solve the problems of basic education of its population  To do this  a fresh understanding of how to approach basic education is needed  requiring a new approach to traditional questions such as the role of the public and private sectors  the links between schools and the communities  pedagogical methods and questions of content  which are discussed in the text
