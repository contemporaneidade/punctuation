In the first part of this article I present theorical contributions from various fields of knowledge concerning gender relations in the classroom among children and adolescents  In the second part  I present partial results from the field research conducted for my Master s dissertation  which took place in a public school in the city of Campinas  Sao Paulo State  The themes I analyze are  children s relations to differences of class  race  gender and other social markers  hierarchies of power in the classroom  and children s concepts on heternormativity
