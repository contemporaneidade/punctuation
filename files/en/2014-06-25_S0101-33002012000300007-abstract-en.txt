The article comments on the recent documental discoveries on  mile Durkheim s oeuvre  and evaluates its impact on the French sociologist s legacy  It aims at enabling the reader to more completely understand the exchange between Durkheim and Salomon Reinach  dedicating special attention to the subject of totemism  one of the fundamental issues of The elementary forms of religious life  published in 1912  and to Durhkeim s involvement with the Dreyfuss affair  which illuminates his political orientation and loyalties
