The article tries to link the history of the book O esp rito das roupas reception to fragments of the author s trajectory  Gilda de Mello e Souza  when coming from fiction to essay  has produced one of the most innovative analyses on fashion  interpreting it as a symbolic language that creates plasticity and expression to diffuse ideas and feelings  This work goes beyond the point of seeing fashion solely as a means of social belonging and distinction  Thus the book  written more than half a century ago  keeps nowadays its vigor
