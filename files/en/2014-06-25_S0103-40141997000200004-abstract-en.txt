This article attempts to draw attention to what has been globalization and avaluate the impact of this phenomenon on Human Rights  Historically  this kind of fundamental legal garantees  known as Human Rights  were created against the Nation State  that is to say  they were conceived to protect citizens against the arbitrariness of public power  However  in so far globalization has undermined the sovereignity of the Nation State and has decreased the coercitive force of the public power  what is the future of Human Rights in this new social and economic context
