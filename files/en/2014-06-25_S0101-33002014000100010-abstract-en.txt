This paper aims to show the central role of economy in O corti o  91 The Slum 93  by Aluisio Azevedo  In this naturalistic novel  the characters are usually presented in accordance with the role they play in the economic life  It is even possible to identify a kind of  accounting  in the preparation and conclusion of several scenes  The vibrating economic life in the space constructed by Jo o Rom o and Bertoleza expresses the complex urban life of Rio de Janeiro during the dramatic transition from slavery to a free labor system  and the rise of Brazilian capitalism  These features can perhaps explain the popularity of this book
