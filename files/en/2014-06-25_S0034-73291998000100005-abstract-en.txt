The article presents a reflection about theory and politics  a reflection that look forward to insert the analysis of Argentina s  Brazil s and Chile s foreign policy in the context of hemispheric relations  with the United States  and of Mercosur  In the case of the three Latin American countries  the article identifies the major tendencies on the interpretation of the possible insertion  of these countries own view in the nowadays international system and a general approximation of the bilateral  sub regional and North American hemispheric agendas
