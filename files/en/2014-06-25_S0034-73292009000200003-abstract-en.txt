This paper analyzes the functioning of the Centro de Informa es do Exterior  CIEX  an agency linked to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs  Itamaraty  and National Intelligence Service  SNI  which was charged with spying political opponents of the Brazilian military regime exiled in neighboring countries  The study aims at examining one component of the repressive system set by the Brazilian dictatorship that had a relative degree of interaction with other military dictatorships in the region of the Southern Cone  The article demonstrates that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs cooperated intensively with the Brazilian military regime
