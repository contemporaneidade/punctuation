Fernando Sabino  Otto Lara Resende  H lio Pellegrino and Paulo Mendes Campos wrote portraits of people with whom they were intimate along their lives by means of short profiles  either in the form of elegies or even of necrologies  Although these texts are necessary in order to set up the style of each one of these writers from Minas Gerais  they also carry the synthesis of each personal experience  which is fundamental for the analysis of the chronicle  a literary genre that the quartet of writers practiced and within which the profiles examined in this essay can be classified
