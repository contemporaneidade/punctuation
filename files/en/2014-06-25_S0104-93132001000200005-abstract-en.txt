Gregory Bateson s most important book  Steps to an Ecology of Mind  was finally re edited in 2000  In the same year  Tim Ingold s book  The Perception of the Environment  which takes up many of Bateson s preocupations  was released  This essay proposes that these two events represent important steps towards an  ecological paradigm  which attempts to answer some of anthropology s dilemas  and open up perspectives for interdisciplinary dialogue on new terms
