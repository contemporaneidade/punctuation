A number of evidence suggests a renewing of national states  interest in acting upon their economies  In Brazil  three new industrial policies were implemented starting in 2003  in addition to the regulatory policies introduced in the 1990s  This poses a challenge of institutional design  how to establish regulatory apparatuses which are economically responsive and densely institutionalized  Understanding the successes of monetary policy might help the elaboration of other economic policies  such as industrial policies  The result of this learning process might translate into credibility and legitimacy gains
