Based on survey results conducted in the Brazilian Presidential elections of 1989  1994  1998 and 2002  and the local election of S o Paulo in 1996  the authors analyse the electoral behavior as a result of a strategy pattern made of three possible choices  the vote as an expression of identity with the candidate  as a belief in the candidate acute s opposition capacity and as a belief in the candidate acute s administrative capacity  The authors point out the link between the vote strategy as an expression of identity with the candidate Lu s In cio da Silva  Lula  in all the period  The article is based on the Brazilian Electoral Study results
