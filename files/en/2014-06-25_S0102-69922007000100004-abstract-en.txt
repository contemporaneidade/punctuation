The present article is about aspects of Benedetto Croce political ideas  It tries to explain concepts expressed in the book Etic and Politic to understand its conection and its contribution to the italian liberal thought  It intends to point the details of Croce s thought about the liberalism  the caracteristics of his State theory  beyond the way of question and think about the liberal concepts of equality  freedom and fraternity  wich were fundamental in the liberal thought compass
