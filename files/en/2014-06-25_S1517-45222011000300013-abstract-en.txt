The work depicts the course of the same name delivered by Michel Foucault at the Coll ge de France  1977 1978  where the author has developed the genealogy of a political knowledge centered on the mechanisms that enable to control people   The government of the self and others  are questioned along a historical course that resulted in a  reason of State  whose rationality entailed in the construction of sets of knowledge and technologies of power  necessary for the growth of State forces  In explaining the problems that the Polizeiwissenschaft should control  he defined the role of the police as a guarantor of internal order and as a technique for controlling people  invested with specific knowledge  and which constitutes  along with the security and the political economy  what Foucault called biopolitics
