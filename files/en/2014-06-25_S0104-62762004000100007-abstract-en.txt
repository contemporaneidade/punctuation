This article identifies the determinants of political trust and its relationship with the support for regime in the relatively recent Latin American democracies  Based on the 1996 Latinobarometer data  the author analyses five hypothesis about political trust in five countries  Argentina  Brasil  Costa Rica  Col mbia and Chile  The results show that the preference for democracy  the conceptions about economy and satisfaction with the government  the optimism with the country and family futures  and the interest in politics are the main determinants of trust in political institutions
