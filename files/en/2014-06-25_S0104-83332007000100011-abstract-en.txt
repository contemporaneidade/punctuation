This article  cased on ethnographic research  documental sources and interviews  focuses on the formation of a gay and lesbian market in S o Paulo since the 1990 s  It analyses a disposition to escape to categories related to sexuality in the definition of spaces of sociability and consumption  which are supported by a public that could be called of  modern  people  The analysis of these processes is founded on theoretical perspectives that assume the contingent and performative character of identity construction  as well as considering the processes related to consumption as basically a social act
