The Mocidade Portuguesa Feminina  an institution created in 1937 by the New State  with the aim of inculcating in children and youth the ideals espoused by the regime  had several publications  including Menina e Mo a  started in 1947  This article examines the culinary concerns in that publication in the years 1947 to 1962  when the Mocidade Portuguesa Feminina celebrated 25 years of existence  We study the information  advice  warnings  and exhortations to the preparation  maintenance  and service of meals  as well as recipes  inserted regularly since 1952 in a section titled  To your cookbook
