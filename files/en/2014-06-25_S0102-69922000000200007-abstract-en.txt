The article analyses the reception of the thought of Roger Bastide in Brazil  during almost two decades  between 1938 and 1954  Bastide  as a teacher  a researcher and a thinker  established different relationships with his contemporaries and his pupils  This essay calls attention to the impact and the importance of Bastide s thoughts in the country  especially in four ex pupils and Brazilian social scientists  Ant nio C ndido  Florestan Fernandes  Gilda de Mello e Souza e Maria Isaura Pereira de Queiroz
