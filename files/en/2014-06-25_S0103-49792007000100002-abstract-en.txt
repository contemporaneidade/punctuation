This paper synthesizes the results of a survey made in nine prestige schools in the city of Rio de Janeiro  Among them  there are confessional  bilingual  public schools and also  alternative  expression used in the sixties for innovative pedagogic projects that emphasized the freedom and autonomy in the students  formation schools  The empiric material we produced offers a panorama regarding the family and school practices that feed themselves in the production of successful institutional paths  on the part of most of the youths that enter and stay at those schools  This is a process of conversion and reconversion of cultural  economical  social  symbolic  academic  capitals quite consistent with the hypothesis of a  virtuous circle  that is  of an optimization of the processes of construction of the teaching quality  in the investigated institutions
