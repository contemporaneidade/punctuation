In a discussion of the patron client relation within Brazilian politics  the article takes as an example political favors asked of Rui Barbosa when he was the provisional government 146 s Minister of the Treasury  It endeavors to demonstrate how the prevalence of patron client values and practices carried over from the monarchy to the Republic  The argument is posed that patronage  from which the patron client relation derives  was deeply embedded in Brazilian society and from there moved into politics  As a consequence  egalitarian values and the notion of res publica  which in thesis should have been an integral part of the new regime 146 s practices  did not find propitious ground upon which to develop  Citizens became  doutor cidad o  while politicians became  cousin and friend
