The aim of this paper is to advance in the analysis about the nature  the meaning and the reach of the Guant namo base  In order to do so  we describe the route of the phenomenon  integrating it in the broader dynamics of the current militarized globalization whose vector and dominating shape go through the imperial strategy of the USA post September 11  Deep articulated with the enlarging logic and consequences of global capitalism  this strategy implies  among other distinctive traces  the legitimation of growing spaces of exceptions  the preventive war without imminent attack threat and the ascension of the  absolute enemy  embodied in the deterritorialized image of the new theological political terrorist
