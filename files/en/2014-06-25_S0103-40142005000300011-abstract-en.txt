THE DEMOCRATIC era in Venezuela dates from 1958  With the Hugo Ch vez Administration  the nation is experiencing a true breach in its national political scene  Therefore it now becomes possible to identify a democratic Venezuela pre  and post Chavez  This article tries to analyze the variables for political and institutional change in Venezuela in four specific moments  first  during the rise and fall of the Punto Fijo Pact  second  the building up of the chavista phenomenon  third  the social and political polarization in Venezuela during Chavez Administration  and finally  Venezuela after the presidential referendum
