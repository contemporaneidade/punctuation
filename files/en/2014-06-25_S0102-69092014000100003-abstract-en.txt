Based on the study of the transformations occurring early this century in private higher education in Brazil  the author proposes to discuss the scope of the concepts of diversity  diversification  innovation  isomorphism  and institutional differentiation  present on the literature on higher education  Would also imply greater diversity and institutional differentiation the fact that the higher education system in Brazil is becoming larger and more complex due to the transformations in the private sector  among them  the emergence of new institutional arrangements and educational modalities  and the presence of international groups
