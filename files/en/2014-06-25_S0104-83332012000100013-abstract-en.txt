This paper aims to analyze women s reading and writing practices in the first half of the 19th century  In this period writing and reading abilities were restricted to a small part of the population  with few indicators of these practices regarding the feminine universe  We analyzed periodicals in circulation during the period  among them O Mentor das Brasileiras  S o Jo o del Rei MG  1829 1832  in which there were texts by female writers and references to women s reading practices
