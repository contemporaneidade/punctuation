Sustainable agriculture should involve the successful management for resources to satisfy changing human needs while maintaining or enhancing the quality of the environment and conserving natural resources  Since ten thousands years ago agriculture developed to several phases  attaining at present an extraordinary efficiency  thanks to the employment of scientific advances  Tropical agriculture presents more problems of higher complexity in comparison to those of temperate climates  In spite of the limitations  tropical Brazilian agriculture is better developed regarding the tropical world in general  Promising new advances are expected from technical innovations  such as Biotechnology
