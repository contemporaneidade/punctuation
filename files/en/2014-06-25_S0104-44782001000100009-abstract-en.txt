The aim of this work is to elucidate the origins of the entrepreneurs  representation crisis in the decade of the 1980 s and the rise of the National Entrepreneurs  Basis  Thinking  Pensamento Nacional das Bases Empresariais   PNBE  in this context  Created in 1987 and institutionalized in 1990  the PNBE was born from a profound dissatisfaction with the answer to the crisis that had been articulated by the traditional entrepreneur s representation structure  Presenting a project aimed to supersede the economic and corporative character of previous propositions of the entrepreneurial class  this entity presented a new alternative of hegemonic character as a solution of the crisis  This project  however  has never assumed defined contours  It was more a faith pledge than a program
