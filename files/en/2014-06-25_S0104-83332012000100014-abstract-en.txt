This paper discusses the many meanings given to the deaths of two poor women in Bel m do Par   Brazil  These women were killed at different times and had the story of their lives and deaths evoked by writers  scholars and the media  as an example to be followed by other women  revealing ideals of fidelity  marriage  family  If in modern days the strength of these stories still emerges with different meanings  the past was not different  indicating the many meanings given to their lives and deaths
