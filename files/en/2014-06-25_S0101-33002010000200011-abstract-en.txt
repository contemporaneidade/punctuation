Zidane  a 21st Century Portrait  a film by Douglas Gordon and Philippe Perreno  belongs to the absorptive tradition that has played a central role in the evolution of modern art  The author explores the tension between absorption and theatricality   crucial to 18th century French art as well as to 20th and 21th century photography   and draws aesthetic and philosophical consequences of this film that depicts  throughout all the 90 minutes of a soccer match  the French midfielder Zinedine Zidane
