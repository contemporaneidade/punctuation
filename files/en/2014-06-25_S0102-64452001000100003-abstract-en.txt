The decentralization principle is found both in the federative organization of the national State and in the basic directions of the health system in Brazil  How does it present itself in each of these dimensions and how are they connected  On the basis of an examination of this question the author analyses the Brazilian national Unitary Health System  Sistema  nico de Sa de   SUS  taking account of its politico institutional context and of its functioning  Basic categories of her analysis are interdependence and autonomy
