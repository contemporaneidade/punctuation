The article presents an analysis of the current configuration of power in the international scenario  divided between the center  hegemonic and not hegemonic  and the periphery  and its subdivisions  At length  a panorama of strategic perceptions of each of the subdivisions of the international system  especially in its relation with Brazil  Argentina and Mercosul  is traced  At the end  the article analyses the current situation and prospects for defense and security policies of Brazil  Argentina and Mercosul
