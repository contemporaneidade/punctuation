In the diagnosis of the modernity  uncertainty and insecurity   and therefore the fear   they are present elements  Leaving of that verification  this work has for objective to describe the ambivalent effects of the fear on the contemporary society  In that attempt  the support of several social theories is looked for that  although they don t focus the fear  they show the constant risks of the modernization process in a certain way  On the other hand  that allows to separate the social critic  that it still tries to continue thinking the modernity with base about the conceptual premises of the western reason  of another one  that for your own fatigue proclaims your self destruction  Understands each other here that to say good bye of the reason and to consider the autonomous movement of the modernity is to do a process that just guarantees the modernization of the fear to appear
