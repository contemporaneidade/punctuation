Le ncio Martins Rodrigues gave this interview on September 10  2007  during a session in the course  Sociology Labor in Brazil  held at the Philosophy and Social Sciences Institute of Rio de Janeiro Federal University  The report approaches aspects of his personal and family life  his political formation  as well as his career as student  professor  and researcher in social sciences  especially relating to themes regarding work  unions  and political parties  It describes his strong connection to both USP and a group of professors  later becoming colleagues who had been under the academic orientation of Florestan Fernandes  It also brings to memory  in detail  procedures and difficulties of various investigations he carried out in factories and union congresses  the later became books and provoked debates
