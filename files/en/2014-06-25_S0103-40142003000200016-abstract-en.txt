This article examines the conflicts involved in circulating in the S o Paulo metropolitan region  more precisely in the city of S o Paulo  The contradictions between the various players  bus company operators  independent van drivers  freighters  motorcyclists  pedestrians and users  are here analyzed  as well as the measures required to assure the feasibility of moving around in the metropolis  Also included is an account of the actions and problems of the municipal government in attempting to reorganize public transportation
