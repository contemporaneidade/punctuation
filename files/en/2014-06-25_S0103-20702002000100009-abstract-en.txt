This paper socialises some of the results of the research entitled Sociology of Adherence  mystical and esoteric practices in the Federal District  that has been developed in the Department of Sociology of the University of Brasilia since 1994  It received financial support from the CNPq  National Research Committee  The research focuses on the mystical esoteric groups in that capital and the region around it  It identifies the main meanings  values  and world view  and highlights the building of a religiousness based on the great interaction between groups  religions  rituals  doctrines and practices  These are fundamental questions for the reflection on the elements around which the search for the sacred is anchored nowadays
