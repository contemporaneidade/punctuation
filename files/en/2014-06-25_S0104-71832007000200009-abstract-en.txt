This article discusses the consumption of Brazilian products   especially fashion and clothing   in France as a means of consuming the exotic  Therefore  the author starts from ten months in fieldwork in France  evaluating the data collected there with the experience from a previous research  carried out in Brazil  Firstly  the article discusses the exoticism  a topic that invariably opens doors to a debate about identities and alterities  Afterwards  some particularities of  consuming the other  are analyzed  present in French discourses concerning Brazilian products  At last  it follows that the construction of an exoticism after the Brazilian fashion is situated simultaneously in the French imaginary about the Brazilian other and in the very Brazilian production of garment fashion
