Through the ethnographic evidence that young children move  or would like to move  between the various churches of the studied city and given that religion seems to be defined by the same children as intrinsically linked to going to church  this article   based on fieldwork research on religion in the North eastern semi arid region of Brazil   works with the hypothesis that the adult reverses the child understanding regarding the meaning given to religion  trying to think of the category symbolic not on its supposed nature or permanence  but as a category constructed during the first years of life of a given person  The data were produced through the use of drawings of children from three to thirteen years of age as research tools  as well as the participant observation
