The article focuses on the recent regulation established for the workday at Volkswagen and Ford  following the dissolution of Autolatina  The agreements introduce the reduction and flexibility of the workday combined with the establishment of a tallying system of the time spent at work  As for Volkswagen  its implementation has an influence over the power relationships and the labour contracts effected by this company  Its results  although quite positive  remain restricted to these car manufacturers  area and the union of metalworkers in the ABC region in S o Paulo  and illustrates its proposition for the union action called  negotiated modernization  This is why these arguments must be appraised in the light of the recent trajectory of the workers and unions  struggles  bearing in mind the trends of the Brazilian and international unions
