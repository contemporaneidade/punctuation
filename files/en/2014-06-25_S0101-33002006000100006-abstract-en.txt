Taking the books O esp rito das roupas e A id ia e o figurado as a guideline  this article analyses the intellectual trajectory of Gilda de Mello e Souza  1919 2005  It shows how the author took advantage of a wide variety of sources  from history to sociology  from anthropology to aesthetics  to create a work full of insights and freedom of expression  and to sharpen her approach to the objects analysed
