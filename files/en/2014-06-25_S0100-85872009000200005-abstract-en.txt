This article presents a reflection on the modalities of sacralization at stake in the movement and the social uses of Catholic rosaries  You take a path that some of the narratives about the origins of traditional religious object  associated with key figures in Catholicism  followed by the presentation of the practices of prayer finally reaching a comparison with other objects considered sacred in Catholicism  In this way  it is emphasized that the procedures of sacralization of the third uses are compatible with both special and ordinary inserted in a process of broad social movements  often performed by non religious ways
