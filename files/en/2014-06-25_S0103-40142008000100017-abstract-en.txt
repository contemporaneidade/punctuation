Starting from the analysis of the political meaning of Brazil and Brazilian in documents written by colonists in the mid 18th century  the article shows the analytical importance of the deviant character of the American variants of the ancien r gime type Portuguese social matrix  Working with the concepts of memory and experience  it points out the idea that  once the Portuguese national structures of both hemispheres became asymmetric  during the revolutionary conjuncture of the 1820s  they were also unable to fit into the same constitutional framework
