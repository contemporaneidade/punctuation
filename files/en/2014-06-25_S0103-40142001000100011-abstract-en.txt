IGN CIO Rangel worked during the main changes of second industrial revolution in Brazil  witnessing the making of the central government as the major institution of that process  and its subsequent crisis  in face of the tension arised by the expansion of monopolist capital  Formed on Marxist stock  Rangel left important contributions to the explanation of the agrarian issue and of inflation  seeing both as brought in by the needs of the strategic needs of capital  He also contributed to the analysis of investment projects  that he dealt as part of planning framework Rangel s work belongs to a transition of the international position of Brazil  Staunchly Brazilian  Rangel moved with a great flair of independence towards the dogmas of his time
