This article examines the impact of party policies on the Brazilian electorate  An important indicator of the consolidation of the party system is the conception of image and party binding among voters  As the party system tends to stabilize  we suppose that voters start fixing the profiles of parties and express party preference or loyalty  It meant that electoral volatility  high in the beginning  would tend to decrease along the time and that  the main parties at least  would constitute their identity  working as  shortcuts  to obtain information on the various different candidacies and for the electoral decision  The main preoccupation of this analysis is to verify at what level such knowledge and party identity construction process have occurred in Brazil  taking as empirical basis a research conducted in the S o Paulo city metro area during the pre electoral period on 2002
