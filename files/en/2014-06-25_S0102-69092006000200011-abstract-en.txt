The work approaches the perception of advanced countries researchers who analyze the transformation in course in the research and higher education institutions concerning the research community s role  vis vis the bureaucrats and entrepreneurs  in their policy making process  Its objective  besides providing an updated panoramic vision on the theme for Latin American colleagues  is to show that many of our community s characteristics seem to be equally present  sometimes in a lessened way  in those countries  The aim of the work is to contribute to a more adequate consideration of these characteristics in the elaboration of the Science and Technology policy of our countries
