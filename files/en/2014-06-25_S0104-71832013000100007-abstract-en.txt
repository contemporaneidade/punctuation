The article explores some information that had been built on an ethnography of a wholesale shop in the neighborhood of Constituci n  where it seeks to both focus and to discuss the relationship between different values   material  moral  ethical and aesthetic   adjacent to the movement of goods and people  Taking as reference the economic anthropology literature  the narrative aims to show how the business operations undertaken  many times in contexts of conflict  promoting the circulation of social values  in which on a daily basis are updated and recorded in a process where the monetary values  the quantities of the coins  the presence or the absence of credit  among others  tell much more than simple exchanges of sale
