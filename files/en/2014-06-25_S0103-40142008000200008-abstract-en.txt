This paper presents and analyzes water resource prospective scenarios from which the National Water Resources Plan has established its strategies  goals and programs  Based on these data the future tendencies for 4 water user sectors with greatest dynamic and conflict potential are analyzed  irrigated agriculture  energy generation  navigation and basic sanitation  Issues which cannot be ignored are the climate changes and how they can affect water supply and demands  From these analyses some conclusions and recommendations are proposed  aiming the anticipation of the future and to prepare public  private and third sector actors to promote rational use of water  focusing on the sustained development  Or  as economist used to say  the optimal allocation of a scarce resource
