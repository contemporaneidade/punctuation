Although most of the studies on political culture stress on attitudes of the population as a whole  some researchers have dedicated their attention to the political values of the national political elite  We believe that the same attention must be paid to the political attitudes of regional political elites  since they operate central parts of the political system  the sub national units of the political system  This article analyses the political opinions of the members of the Paran  state parliament  based on data collected by a survey  We intend to indicate the importance of the  ideological position  variable to understand the differences in the intensity of the adhesion to the democratic system between the right and left wing members of the Paran  state parliament
