This article presents some reflections on the social construction of homosexual conjugality in contemporary Brazil  particularly concerning the Legislative sphere   where most of the debate on the law project for civil partnership between people of the same sex has taken place  The main objective is to analyze the structuring elements of the ideological struggles stemming from the disputes around the social and juridical recognition of homosexual unions as familial entities  The discourses that come from the Catholic Church and those of homosexual representatives are also given priority in the analysis
