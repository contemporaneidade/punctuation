The purpose of this article is to discuss in the light of Arthur Ramos  work  particularly concerning anthropological studies of Afro Brazilian religions  the reason why this Brazilian author dedicated so little attention to the Shango of his country  making reference to the persecution suffered by worship houses of Macei  city  in 1912  only superficially and without the concern to investigate the motivations of the episode which was called by its practitioners  Operation Shango
