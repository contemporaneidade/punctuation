The aim of the present article is to investigate the music from Santo Daime  religion especially among members and leaders from both churches that belong to  Padrinho Sebasti o Mota de Melo s spiritual line  in the city of Rio de Janeiro  More specifically  I analyze the native interpretation on genesis of the religious hymns in its relation with the thoughts  feelings and subjectivity  reflected in the notion of sacred and the hierarchic differentiations of the group  The theoretical support for the analysis is anchored in the fields of ethnomusicology and anthropology of emotions
