The text undertakes a moving historical appraisal of the dramatic experiences faced by a family of migrants  the Parondi family  through their move from the rural south of Italy to the industrial metropolis of Milan  pointing out the expressive conflicting lives of the characters the mother  the five male siblings  their women   just as performed in the classic feature film by neo realist Italian director Luchino Visconti  Rocco and his brothers  Rocco i suoi fratelli  of 1960
