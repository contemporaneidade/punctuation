The text deals with the French Police System crisis making references to the different ways of acting of the National Police and the National Guard by defining the areas of activities of each one  Under the context of the European unification  the author shows the development of the private security services and the disputes between them and those ones supported by the government  The text also shows that the specificities of the modern time as the drug traffic and terrorism demand for specific police action  This specificity  however  cannot mean to leave the daily civil security
