Information about Afro Brazilian religions  candombl  and umbanda  often reaches the general public through the phonographic industry  radio and television  Brazilian Popular Music  MPB  is also an important source of information about these religions  For this reason this paper attempts to interpret the ways in which the values from these religions appear on MPB  having as an empirical field the artistic production of Clara Nunes  analyzes of lyrics  performances in concerts  video clips and appearances in TV shows  as well as the symbols chosen to disseminate her work  shown in LP covers and inner sleeves
