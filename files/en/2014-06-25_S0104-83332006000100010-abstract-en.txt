The aim of this paper is to show what has been published on child sexual violence by O Estado de S Paulo  a well sold Brazilian daily newspaper  The research covered the 20th century and the many forms of sexual violence  incest  crimes committed by strangers  honour crimes  prostitution  child pornography and paedophilia  The data will be presented in two parts  the first one covering the beginning of the century and the second one the last decades  On doing this  I focus on how sexual crimes were reported and  also  on the social sensibility that can be drawn from the news  The main changes on how the newspaper talks about sexual violence are analyzed in the conclusion
