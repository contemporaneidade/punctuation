Epidemics must not be seen as a special kind of disease but as part of a complex process of distribution of pathologies over time and space  Among the different existing disease classifications  the ICD  currently in its 10th edition  is the official one  The control charts being employed by the health system allow estimating the stage of development  endemic or epidemic  of diseases  especially those subject to worldwide control  the quarantinable diseases  The concept outbreak should be understood as a signal for the concentration of episodes worth being studied in depth  The most important communicable diseases in the developing world  the so called neglected diseases  deserve special attention from the research funding agencies for not ranging among the priorities of the pharmaceutical industry  In Brazil  the agenda of priorities in health research includes various diseases of this nature  which have been contemplated in calls for projects launched by the National research Council and by the Ministry of Health
