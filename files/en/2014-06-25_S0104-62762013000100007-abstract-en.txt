After 20 years of reestablished democracy in Brazil  what do the state legislators think about the regime  The goal of the article is to show the views of state legislators of the Legislative Assemblies of the States of S o Paulo and Paran  on democracy  For the views we use a structured and self administered questionnaire  The results show that for the state legislators  i  democracy is stable  ii  it is superior to any other kind of regime  iii  it s support comes from some public policy and the use of voting and iv  parties and elections are important for democracy
