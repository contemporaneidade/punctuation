 Traditional  museums have their center of gravity in collections that often precede the institutions themselves  It is the classic model of private collections that meet the public institutional channels and accessibility  The Museum of Zoology of the University of S o Paulo is not an exception to this rule  Its collection is linked to collections of the nineteenth century incorporated to the State of S o Paulo  in comings and goings of institutional settings to the current status as university museum of excellence in research  teaching and outreach  As such  MZUSP continues to face challenges and changing needs and adapt to new realities of the twenty first century
