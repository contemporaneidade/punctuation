A short summary of the central questions regarding the teaching of Sociology in Brazilian secondary schools  the intermittent character of the teaching  the fragmentation of the researches  the difficulties in the consolidation of minimum contents and didactic materials  Based on a recent bibliography and on my own experience as Professor of Teaching Methodology of Social Sciences  I relate these difficulties  on the one hand  to the distance and hierarchization that exist between the social scientists as academic intellectuals and the social scientists as secondary teachers and  on the other  to the divorce and unbalance existing between the educational development of bachelors in social science and those taking Diploma in Education within the model in effect since the faculties of philosophy  sciences and letters were created in the 1930  s
