This article is a essay to understand the limits of the autonomy of intellectual life in S o Paulo  in the early years of the 20th century  Following the track from the modernist writer Oswald de Andrade  his participation in the bohemian groups from Rio de Janeiro and in the modernist group from S o Paulo in 1922  we will analyze how works the process of institutionalization of culture in terms of building a new pattern to permit recognition and legitimization for intellectual life
