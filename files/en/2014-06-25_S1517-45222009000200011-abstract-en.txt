This article intends to draw a socioeconomic and educational profile of the deputies of the state of Paran  from 1995 to 2006  following suggestions found in literature  Next  the paper considers the hypothesis that there is some relationship between the ideological position of the political parties  left right  and the social origin of the surveyed deputies  within the context of Paran  To conclude  the article provides suggestions for research  which  if they are implemented  would further advance the study of the regional and national political elites
