In this article the political competition in Venezuela is analysed in order to evaluate its possibilities to stablish a relatively stable party system and to organize the Venezuelan political life  It studies the level of ideological structuration and programmatic distinctions of some parties related to other parties and groups  as dimensions that contributes to the citizens  electoral choice  The data become from the research  Parliamentary elites in Latin America  directed by Manuel Alc ntara S ez  in the Institute for Iberoamerica and Portugal Studies  Universidad de Salamanca
