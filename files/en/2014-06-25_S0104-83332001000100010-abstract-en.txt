The objectives of this study are  1  To review the proposals of philanthropic entities  organized and managed by women in the first half of the 20th century  2  To rethink the role they played elaborating programs and establishing public policies  3  To call attention to the need of reviewing the Brazilian feminist movement  The guide for these topics will be based on the analysis of the maternalist discourse  The research was based on documents from the archives of the Cruzada Pr  Inf ncia  Crusade Pro Childhood  an entity founded in the city of Sao Paulo in 1930  It will focus on the proposals for maternity protection elaborated by P rola Byington  its managing director between 1930 1945
