This text explores  analytically and empirically  the problem of alienation and  or  estrangement  seeking to help in the intellection of the socio historical foundations that provide it to flourish within the modern capitalist enterprise  To do so it performs the following movements  first  starting with Marx s seminal theoretical concepts  it attempts to indicate the different forms assumed by the alienaton and  or  estrangement throughout the twentieth century and early twenty first century  exploring some of its main features  In its second motion  it outlines a phenomenology of subjectivity  exploring  as a hypothesis  the rich analytical possibilities existing in Luk cs s mature work  in particular his notions of innocent reification and stranged reification  fertile as clues to understand this social phenomenon in capitalism today
