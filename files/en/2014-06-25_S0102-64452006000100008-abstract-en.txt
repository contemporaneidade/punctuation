The paper addresses the main issue of political theory  legitimacy  The theme is developed beginning with the basic concepts of the political philosophy of the Enlightenment  individualism  liberalism and philosophy of history  with the help of the conceptual historiography of Reinhart Koselleck  to point out a political aporia  The specific articulation between those concepts is then used to read the main works of Friedrich August von Hayek  as an example of the adaptation of that framework to neoliberalism and  on the other hand  to read the core of the work of J rgen Habermas  as an example of the social democratic version  The conclusion points to the systems theory of Niklas Luhmann as a possible alternative to the political aporia inherited from the basic conceptual framework of the Enlightenment
