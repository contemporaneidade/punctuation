Starting from a general reflection on the study of  material culture  in this article I experiment the analytic profitability of the theoretical formulations of Igor Kopytoff  author who suggested a research of the cultural biography of the objects  emphasizing their life stages and the gradations  overlaps and recurrences of classifications that cause their vulgarization or singularization in a given society  And searching a creative dialogue with this author  I use notes taken during a fieldwork with members of the carnival group Afox  Filhos de Gandhi of Rio de Janeiro  composed of practitioners of Candomble and who used ritually  baskets  during a religious procession in the streets of the city named Yemanja s Gift
