This article aims at providing a possible reading of the reality of racism in Brazil in which laughter plays a relevant mediating role  Inserted among distinct realities   psychic and social  conscious and unconscious  playful and serious   the laughter  as a by product of racist jokes  can articulate these realities in such a manner as to contribute to concealing and consolidating the existing racism in Brazil  Its effect may be obscured as a consequence of a public debate in which laughter itself becomes the object of the discussion
