FOR MANY reasons  women go to the labour market  emancipation  leading to professional plans  income loss due to spouse s unemployment or other changes  increases in the number of women heading families  However  it has been an uneasy transition  as in the current conjunctural context people looking for a job are experiencing all sort of barriers  Another dificulties even worse women conditions in the labour market  They have to do with gender discrimination  which leads to low salaries  inequality in employment access and opportunities  unemployment  To better show this situation  Dieese gathered and analysed the results of collective bargaining in our country  from 1996 to 2000  in the gender context  The results show how far from equality we are referring to gender issues  and the relevance of negotiation as an instrument of collective regulamentation in labour relations
