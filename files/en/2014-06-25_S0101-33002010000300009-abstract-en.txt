The article offers a comparative analysis of the educational policies of president Fernando Henrique Cardoso  1995 2003  taking as reference the period between 1992 2008  It demonstrates the great progress towards the universalization of basic education  the expansion of higher education and the reforms based on a new educational law  It also analyses the importance of the implementation of a comprehensive system of evaluation of all levels of education  Finally it analyzes the problems related to the improvement of education quality
