On the eve of the International Social Conference in Copenhagen  1995  the author defends new strategies  suitable for a development axiology  According to him  there is no development without a balanced growth within each country and in the North South relations  On the other hand  one cannot ignore ecology  Finally  the target of development is man  his own culture  his universal values  the right to a productive insertion and to the participation as a citizen  This pressuposes a democratic State  regulator of a mixed economy  At the juridic level  the author suggests the creation of an independent international forum
