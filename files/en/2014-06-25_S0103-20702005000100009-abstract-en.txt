Les H ritiers  les  tudiants et la culture  written by Pierre Bourdieu in partnership with Jean Claude Passeron and published in Paris in 1964  ditions de Minuit  was the result of research done through a questionnaire  based on a sample of students of Letters  in Paris and from a few other French universities  Pierre Bourdieu taught sociology at the University of Lille  North  the statement published herwith tells about the conditions under which the research was undertaken in Lille  under his supervision  and retraces the history of the work  from its elaboration at the European Sociology Centre  to the reception inside and outside university research environment
