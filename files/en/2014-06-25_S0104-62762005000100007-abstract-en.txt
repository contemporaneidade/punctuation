This paper analyzes the work done by media in the making of social representations upon the growing of genetically modified  GM  crops and food in Rio Grande do Sul  showing its role in the increase of political polarization on this issue in the state  In order to demonstrate this  two events in the towns of Cachoeirinha and N o Me Toque are considered  The type of coverage given by the media to these events is apprehended to enable the observation of the local media role in the building of social representations concerning GM organisms
