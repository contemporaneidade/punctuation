This article aims to identify and analyze different aspects of masculinity presented in the literature of Bernardo Guimar es  an important 19th century Brazilian writer from Minas Gerais  We shall focus how the author  in his literary work  constructs the gender relations and produces representations about men and women  specially the masculine experiences  without forgetting the feminine ones  We intend  in the same way  to contribute to a broader understanding of the participation of literature in the production of representations and practices of masculinity  and of femininity  in late nineteenth century Brazil
