This article brings a specific gaze into women s world  The locus of our study is the city of Campina Grande  Para ba  from 1930 to 1945  emphasising the meanings and implications of some activities carried out by working class women through the city  and the ways they used its spaces and territories  We also seek to rebuild everyday aspects of their trajectories through streets and suburbs  focusing singular characteristics of anonymous women who appeared at the urban scene turning it into a place of multiple practices  relations and conflicts
