This article seeks to associate the theoretical devices related to the center periphery theme  multi level governance  federalism and positive negative integration to the very reality of subnational unities within Mercosur  Some field inquiries with economical agents and political actors are subsequently considered in order to favor primary sources of information  Finally this text proposes to scrutinize the impact of the Southern Common Market on the political economy of Pernambuco  Bahia  S o Paulo  Rio Grande do Sul and their respective capitals
