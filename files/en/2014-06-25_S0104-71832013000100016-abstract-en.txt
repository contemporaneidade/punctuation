This text is about the process of translation  The text is an anthropology text and the translator is an anthropologist  The ethnography O Vapor do Diabo  o trabalho dos oper rios do a car  The Devil s Steam  work in a sugar factory  was written by Jos  Sergio Leite Lopes in 1976  The research required to undertake this particular translation is underlined and the author s participation in this process is made central  By describing key decisions in the process it is argued that ethnographies should be translated by anthropologists
